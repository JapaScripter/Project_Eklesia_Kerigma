About us

God's dream!

Adalto da Silva Almeida, converted at 22, married Daiene P. B. de Almeida at 24. At 26, he received the opportunity to study theology full-time with his wife at the Theological School of Southern Brazil (FATESB), now the Theological Seminary of Southern Brazil (SETESB), in Vargem Grande Paulista, São Paulo. He graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Theology after having his theological course validated by the Faculty of Integrated Theology (FATIN). He also completed a postgraduate degree in missiology at Unyleya Faculty. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Portuguese.

He was consecrated to the presbytery in 2007 and to the pastorate in 2019. In 2013, after graduating, he was invited by the presiding pastor, Pastor Joanir de Moura, to pastor congregations, becoming part of the full-time group of pastors at the Conselheiro Assembly of God Peña Ministry, Minas Gerais. He remained in this position until the end of 2019.

This marks the beginning of a new stage in Pastor Adalto's life. Responding to the call to carry out cross-cultural missions, he received an invitation from the ministry to go to the mission field to support another missionary on the field in Bolivia, remaining in that country for a year and five months.

In July 2021, he was transferred to Paraguay to work on the revitalization of a church in the city of Hernandarias, in the department of Alto Paraná, where he currently resides.
