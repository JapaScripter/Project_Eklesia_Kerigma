God's purpose

Values

Guided by biblical values, we seek to live with justice and truth, obeying God's commandments, maintaining honesty, expressing love for others, respecting all those around us, practicing humility, and reaching out in mercy. Through prayer and worship, we always remember that we are all equal before God.

Mission

Evangelize, Win, Consolidate, Disciple and Send.

Vision

May everyone know Jesus Christ. Our purpose is to guide people to a deeper relationship with God, cultivate love and service to others, and make Jesus known to all nations, driven by the power of the Holy Spirit and dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission.
